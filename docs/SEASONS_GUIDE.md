# Seasons Guide

## Overview

Life operates in four distinct seasons, each with specific purposes, rules, and risks. Seasons must rotate deliberately with minimum duration requirements.

## Season Details

### Spring (Planning & Preparation)

**Provides:**
- Fresh energy
- New ideas
- Growth potential

**Asks:**
- Strategic planning
- Skill building
- Relationship nurturing

**Focus Areas:**
- Learning
- Networking
- System building

**Rules:**
- No major commitments
- No expansion

**Risks:**
- Over-planning without action

**XP Multiplier:** 1.2x for Learning/Planning activities

**Minimum Duration:** 4 weeks

---

### Summer (Peak Output)

**Provides:**
- Maximum capacity
- High energy
- Opportunity

**Asks:**
- Focused execution
- High-value work
- Relationship investment

**Focus Areas:**
- Revenue generation
- Project completion
- Growth

**Rules:**
- Protect capacity
- Maintain boundaries

**Risks:**
- Burnout
- Overcommitment

**XP Multiplier:** 1.3x for Work/Revenue activities

**Minimum Duration:** 4 weeks

---

### Autumn (Harvest & Consolidation)

**Provides:**
- Results from summer
- Stability
- Clarity

**Asks:**
- Optimization
- System refinement
- Knowledge sharing

**Focus Areas:**
- Asset building
- Process improvement
- Teaching

**Rules:**
- No new major initiatives

**Risks:**
- Complacency
- Missed opportunities

**XP Multiplier:** 1.2x for Optimization/Teaching activities

**Minimum Duration:** 4 weeks

---

### Winter (Rest & Recovery)

**Provides:**
- Rest
- Reflection
- Deep work

**Asks:**
- Minimal output
- Recovery
- Planning

**Focus Areas:**
- Health
- Relationships
- Strategic thinking

**Rules:**
- **NO major bets**
- **NO expansion**
- **NO high-stakes decisions**

**Risks:**
- Guilt about rest
- Pressure to produce

**XP Multiplier:** 1.1x for Rest/Reflection activities (lower but sustainable)

**Minimum Duration:** 4 weeks

**Automatic Trigger:** If Water < 20, system forces Winter season

---

## Season Transition Rules

### Validation Rules

1. **Minimum Duration:** Must stay in current season for at least 4 weeks
2. **Water Level Check:** If Water < 20, can only transition to Winter
3. **Water Level Block:** If Water < 30, cannot transition to Summer
4. **Winter Exit:** Can only transition from Winter to Spring

### Transition Process

1. User requests season transition
2. System validates transition rules
3. If valid:
   - Record current season in history
   - Update user's current season
   - Reset season start date
   - Log transition reason

---

## Season-Specific Guidance

### Spring Activities
- Take courses
- Read books
- Network and build relationships
- Plan projects
- Build systems and processes

### Summer Activities
- Execute high-value projects
- Generate revenue
- Complete major work
- Invest in relationships
- Push boundaries (safely)

### Autumn Activities
- Optimize existing systems
- Teach and share knowledge
- Build assets
- Refine processes
- Consolidate gains

### Winter Activities
- Rest and recover
- Reflect on past season
- Plan for next cycle
- Focus on health
- Nurture relationships
- Deep work (low pressure)

---

## Season Completion Bonus

Completing a full season cycle (all 4 seasons) awards:
- +1,000 Overall XP
- +200 XP to each category

---

## Best Practices

1. **Respect the Seasons:** Don't fight against your current season
2. **Plan Transitions:** Think ahead about when to transition
3. **Track Duration:** Monitor days in season
4. **Follow Rules:** System rules exist to protect you
5. **Embrace Winter:** Rest is productive, not lazy
